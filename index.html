About the creators

The 'Get Together for Ukraine' foundation is located in The Hague and was established in June 2022 with the aim of raising money for acute emergency aid to the victims of the war in Ukraine. The foundation organizes house concerts/benefit concerts with performances by talented, young Ukrainian musicians and artists who had to leave Ukraine because of the war and now reside in our country as refugees.

The money we raise is donated to local charities in Ukraine that operate in the areas hardest hit by the war and where humanitarian aid is most needed. Everyone who works for our foundation does so as a volunteer and of course without any compensation.

We are extremely grateful for every contribution to this project and we thank you in advance for your involvement and support!
